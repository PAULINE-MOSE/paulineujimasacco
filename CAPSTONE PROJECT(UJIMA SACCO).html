Ethics, Governance & Sovereignty

Principled AI by Design

Four interlocking frameworks ensuring Ujima AI SACCO operates with conscience, transparency, and human dignity at every layer.

ETHOS Framework
Conscience Compass — Calibrating Moral GPS
EEmpathy — Who might be harmed by this output? Vulnerable groups identified before every deployment.
TTransparency — Can you trace how the AI reached this conclusion? Full reasoning chain exposed.
HHuman Impact — What real-world consequence does this decision trigger? Food security. Education. Dignity.
OOwnership — Who is accountable when things go wrong? Human officer, not algorithm, bears responsibility.
SSovereignty — Whose data, culture, and dignity are we respecting? African-generated data stays in Africa.
TRACK Framework
Bias Detection — Tracking Invisible Predators
TTraining Data — Audit whose voices dominate the dataset. Ensure diverse, representative African data.
RRepresentation — Market vendors, shea butter traders, small farmers — all occupation categories included.
AAmplification — Actively test whether AI magnifies historical inequalities in lending patterns.
CCounterfactuals — "What if address = Nairobi?" Systematic identity-swapping tests deployed quarterly.
KKill Switch — Immediate override for any district with approval disparity >40%.
OASIS Data Protocol
Sacred Watering Hole — Data Sovereignty First
OOpt-In Explicit Consent — Swahili-language consent scripts. Never assume. Always ask like a respectful guest.
AComplete Anonymization — K-anonymity pipelines preventing re-identification of any individual member.
SSovereign Cloud Storage — AWS Africa (Cape Town) region. Kenya DPA 2022 compliant at all times.
IIntentional Retention — 90-day auto-delete for non-essential metadata. Hoarding is hubris.
SSecurity as Ritual — Signal Protocol encryption. Daily practice, not compliance checkbox.
PRIDE Governance Loop
Human-in-the-Loop — No Lion Hunts Alone
PPause Points — Mandatory human review for income variance >30%, high-value loans, and flagged anomalies.
RReview Cadence — Quarterly bias audits with CBK, scheduled like seasonal migrations.
IInterpretability — Explanations in plain Swahili: "Your loan was paused due to seasonal income variance."
DDisagreement Rights — USSD *#123# lets any member instantly request human review without penalty.
EElders Council — SACCO managers, women farmers, fintech regulators, and community leaders govern the system.
